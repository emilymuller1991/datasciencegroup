Objectives

Wed Dec 27, 2017 by Data Science Group, AIMS, South Africa

The key object is to empower female students at AIMS South Africa to achieve their full potential. Furthermore, AIMSWIS intends to train a generation of women scientists as mentors to increase the number of role models for girls in African communities. Finally, AIMSWIS wishes to create a space for women students in mathematical sciences.

About

Wed Dec 27, 2017 by Data Science Group, AIMS, South Africa

Women are historically underrepresented in the Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) professions worldwide; however, the situation is worse in Africa. Since its establishment in 2003, the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) has succeeded in increasing the representation and retention of women in STEM by recruiting more women students at its centers. Since 2003 until present, 1523 students from 42 countries have graduated from AIMS, of which 34% are women. Read more →
